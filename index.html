About Me

My name is Morgan Manning. I am originally from Columbia, South Carolina. I currently reside in Miami, Florida. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Film & Media Arts from American University in Washington, DC. I am pursuing a Master of Arts in Journalism from University of Miami. I will graduate with a M.A. in Journalism in May 2018.

I am interested in doing video journalism or data visualization. I have learned I have a passion for doing video and data visualization for journalistic stories. My website showcases some of the work I have done during my graduate studies.
